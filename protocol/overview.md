# RAY token

This page covers the core tokenomics of RAY, including supply, emissions, fee-based buybacks, and allocation transparency.

## At a glance

- RAY has a fixed maximum supply (hard cap) of 555 million tokens.
- The mining reserve accounts for 34% of the total supply, or 188.7 million RAY.
- 12% of all trading fees generated by the protocol are used to buy back RAY.

## RAY mint address

## Circulating supply and emissions

The majority of the remaining locked supply is allocated to the mining reserve, partnership and ecosystem, and advisor allocations.

The protocol currently emits approximately 1.9 million RAY per year from the mining reserve.

## Locked token distribution

- Team and seed allocations, representing 25.9% of the total supply, were fully locked for the first 12 months following the token generation event (TGE).
- Tokens then unlocked linearly on a daily basis from months 13 through 36.
- All vesting concluded on February 21, 2024.
