# Overview

Compare Raydium's core liquidity products and see how CLMM, CPMM, AMM v4, and LaunchLab fit together.

Raydium supports multiple liquidity pool designs rather than a single AMM model. Each product is designed for a different mix of simplicity, flexibility, and capital efficiency.

## Pool models

- [CLMM](/overview/core-products/clmm/what-is-clmm): Concentrated liquidity for active LPs who want precise range control and higher capital efficiency.
- [CPMM](/overview/core-products/cpmm/what-is-cpmm): Full-range constant product liquidity for simple, passive, and integration-friendly pool design.
- [AMM v4](/overview/core-products/amm-v4/what-is-amm-v4): Raydium's legacy constant product AMM with broad historical adoption across the ecosystem.

## How to think about them

- `CLMM` is best when you want concentrated liquidity and active position management.
- `CPMM` is best when you want simplicity and standard full-range liquidity.
- `AMM v4` is best when you need compatibility with Raydium's legacy pool model.

Raydium also supports products on top of these pools, including LaunchLab for token launches and supporting products like Ecosystem Farms and Burn and Earn.
