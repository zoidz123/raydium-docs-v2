# Core products

Raydium supports multiple pool designs rather than a single AMM. Each product trades off simplicity, flexibility, capital efficiency, and post-launch behavior in a different way.

## In this section

- [CLMM](/overview/core-products/clmm): Concentrated liquidity for active LPs who want precise range control and higher capital efficiency.
- [CPMM](/overview/core-products/cpmm): Full-range constant product liquidity for simple, passive, and integration-friendly pool design.
- [AMM v4](/overview/core-products/amm-v4): Raydium's legacy constant product AMM with broad historical adoption across the ecosystem.
- [LaunchLab](/overview/core-products/launchlab): Token launch infrastructure built around bonding curves and AMM migration.

## How to choose

### CLMM

Best when you want capital efficiency and you are comfortable managing price ranges more actively.

### CPMM

Best when you want simple, full-range liquidity without active range management.

### AMM v4

Best when you need Raydium's legacy constant product model and its established ecosystem footprint.

### LaunchLab

Best when you are creating a token launch flow that starts on a bonding curve and graduates into Raydium liquidity.
