# CLMM

CLMM is Raydium's concentrated liquidity model. It increases capital efficiency by letting LPs concentrate liquidity where trading activity is most likely to happen, but it also requires more active management than full-range pools.

## Start here

- [What is CLMM](/overview/core-products/clmm/what-is-clmm)
- [CLMM fees](/overview/core-products/clmm/fees)
- [Estimated APR for CLMM](/overview/core-products/clmm/estimated-apr)

## Best for

- Stable or correlated pairs
- LPs who actively manage price ranges
- Strategies that prioritize capital efficiency over simplicity
