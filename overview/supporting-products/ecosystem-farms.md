# Ecosystem Farms

Ecosystem farms are incentive programs that let projects add token rewards to eligible Raydium pools. They are used to attract liquidity and encourage participation around a pool or token.

## What it does

- Adds external token incentives to liquidity pools
- Encourages users to deposit and stay in liquidity positions
- Helps projects grow liquidity around a market

## Who it is for

Ecosystem farms are primarily for token teams and pool operators that want to drive liquidity growth with reward programs.
