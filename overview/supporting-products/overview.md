# Supporting products

These products are built on top of Raydium pools and token flows. They are useful when you want to deepen liquidity, align communities around a pool, or add reward mechanics on top of existing markets.

## In this section

- [Ecosystem Farms](/overview/supporting-products/ecosystem-farms): External token incentives for eligible Raydium pools.
- [Burn and Earn](/overview/supporting-products/burn-and-earn): Permanent liquidity locking while preserving fee rights.

## How to think about them

### Ecosystem Farms

Best when a project wants to drive participation and liquidity through reward emissions.

### Burn and Earn

Best when a team wants to lock liquidity permanently while still retaining fee participation.
