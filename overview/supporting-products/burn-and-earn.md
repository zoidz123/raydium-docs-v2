# Burn and Earn

Burn and Earn is a Raydium product that lets liquidity be locked permanently while preserving access to the trading fees generated by that liquidity position.

## What it does

- Locks liquidity permanently into supported pools
- Keeps the liquidity in market
- Preserves fee rights through a transferable fee key NFT

## Who it is for

Burn and Earn is for teams and liquidity providers that want to signal long-term commitment to a pool while still keeping fee participation.
