# Trading

Raydium supports both token swaps and leveraged perpetual futures. Use this section to understand which trading surface fits your workflow before diving into the mechanics of each product.

## In this section

- [Raydium Swap](/overview/trading/raydium-swap): Spot token swaps routed across available liquidity.
- [Raydium Perps](/overview/trading/raydium-perps): Leveraged perpetual futures trading on top of Orderly infrastructure.

## Which product fits your use case?

### Raydium Swap

Use it when you want direct spot execution against Raydium liquidity pools with a simple front-end workflow.

### Raydium Perps

Use it when you want leveraged exposure, perpetual futures markets, and a more advanced trading interface.
