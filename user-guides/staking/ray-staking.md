# RAY staking

Stake RAY to earn additional RAY rewards.

## How to stake

1. Navigate to the **Staking** tab in the Raydium app.

![Open the staking tab in the Raydium app](https://4071094211-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2F-MT-qzD26DX_h6l_vXaA-887967055%2Fuploads%2F1vYegvmISNoCxsMKxftx%2FScreenshot%202026-01-13%20at%205.08.07%E2%80%AFPM.png?alt=media&token=18f5fd8c-0db1-4713-85ec-89f5ee288394)

2. Enter the amount to stake, click **Stake**, and approve the transaction.

![Confirm the stake in the Raydium staking screen](https://4071094211-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2F-MT-qzD26DX_h6l_vXaA-887967055%2Fuploads%2FKKEgKqAQpdA0Hn4WvJWW%2FScreenshot%202026-01-13%20at%205.08.57%E2%80%AFPM.png?alt=media&token=3d92cdd5-88b5-4631-800b-a20b6e4cc2fa)

3. Once staked, you'll see your staked balance and pending rewards.

## How to unstake

1. Navigate to the staking page or the portfolio page.
2. Click the minus button, confirm, and sign the transaction.
