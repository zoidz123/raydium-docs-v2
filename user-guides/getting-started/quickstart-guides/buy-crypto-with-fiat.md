# How to buy crypto with fiat

Raydium supports fiat on-ramp and off-ramp flows through [MoonPay](https://www.moonpay.com/).

## Buy crypto with fiat

1. Go to the swap page.
2. Click the `$` button.
3. Follow the MoonPay checkout flow.

![](https://4071094211-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2F-MT-qzD26DX_h6l_vXaA-887967055%2Fuploads%2F2NvwlHBF4IDmA39jv7uG%2FCapture%20d%E2%80%99e%CC%81cran%202024-07-30%20a%CC%80%2012.08.09.png?alt=media&token=334324e4-7120-4cb2-b699-81943e9eaa4c)

## Sell crypto for fiat

1. Connect your wallet.
2. Click your wallet address.
3. Select **Off-ramp with MoonPay**.

![](https://4071094211-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2F-MT-qzD26DX_h6l_vXaA-887967055%2Fuploads%2FofV4fBoA1f11OHsRbBXF%2FCapture%20d%E2%80%99e%CC%81cran%202024-07-30%20a%CC%80%2012.17.15.png?alt=media&token=88d8d049-b900-461e-a144-254f1a6e5f35)
