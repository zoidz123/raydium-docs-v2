# How to set up a wallet

A wallet stores your funds and lets you interact with Raydium. Keep at least `0.05 SOL` in your wallet to avoid failed transactions.

## Recommended wallets

| Wallet                            | Platforms             |
| --------------------------------- | --------------------- |
| [Phantom](https://phantom.com/)   | Browser, iOS, Android |
| [Solflare](https://solflare.com/) | Browser, iOS, Android |
| [Backpack](https://backpack.app/) | Browser               |
| [Jupiter](https://jup.ag/mobile)  | Browser, iOS, Android |

Already have a wallet elsewhere? Import your 12 or 24-word seed phrase into one of the supported wallets above.

## Safety checks

- Download wallets from official websites or app stores only.
- Back up your seed phrase offline.
- Never share your seed phrase or private key.
- Review every transaction in your wallet before you approve it.
