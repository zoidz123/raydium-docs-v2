# How to earn fees

## Share a referral link

Click **Share** on any token page to copy your unique referral link. Your wallet must be connected.

## How it works

When anyone trades through your link, you earn **0.1%** of every trade. Fees are paid in SOL or the pool's quote token and are automatically airdropped to your wallet.

Your link points to a specific token, but you will also earn if users trade other LaunchLab tokens in the same session.
