# How to stake into farms

Stake LP tokens or hold in-range CLMM positions to earn farm rewards on top of trading fees.

This guide explains how rewards work for both constant product farms and CLMM farms.

## How farms work

| Pool type | How rewards work |
| --------- | ---------------- |
| **Constant product (CPMM)** | Stake your LP tokens in a farm to earn rewards. LP tokens must be unstaked before you remove liquidity. |
| **Concentrated liquidity (CLMM)** | Rewards are distributed automatically to in-range positions. No staking is required. |

For CLMM pools, you earn farm rewards simply by holding an in-range position.

## Stake LP tokens for constant product farms

### Step 1: Add liquidity

Before staking, you need LP tokens. Add liquidity to a constant product pool to receive LP tokens that represent your share of the pool.

1. Go to **Liquidity** and select a pool.
2. Deposit equal value of both tokens.
3. Confirm the transaction.
4. Wait for LP tokens to appear in your wallet.

### Step 2: Find the farm

1. Navigate to the **Portfolio** page.
2. Open the **Standard** tab.
3. Look for pools with an active **Farm** indicator.

Alternatively, browse all available farms on the **Liquidity** page and filter by **Farms**.

### Step 3: Stake LP tokens

1. Click your LP position.
2. Click **Stake**.
3. Enter the amount of LP tokens to stake, or click **Max**.
4. Confirm the transaction in your wallet.

Your staked balance and pending rewards will now appear in your portfolio.

### Step 4: Harvest rewards

1. Go to **Portfolio**.
2. Find your staked position.
3. Click **Harvest** to claim pending rewards.

Rewards are sent directly to your wallet.

### Step 5: Unstake LP tokens

To remove liquidity, you must first unstake your LP tokens:

1. Go to **Portfolio**.
2. Click your staked position.
3. Click **Unstake**.
4. Enter the amount to unstake.
5. Confirm the transaction.

After unstaking, you can remove liquidity from the pool.

## Earn rewards on CLMM pools

For concentrated liquidity pools, farm rewards are distributed automatically. No staking is required.

### Requirements to earn

* Your position must be **in range**.
* The pool must have an active farm with emissions.

### Check farm status

* Hover over the reward token symbol to see the reward period.
* Farms display **Upcoming** before the start time.

### Claim rewards

1. Go to **Portfolio**.
2. Find your CLMM position.
3. Review pending rewards and unclaimed fees.
4. Click **Harvest** to claim both fees and rewards.

## Important notes

* **Staked LP tokens still earn trading fees.** Fees compound into the value of your LP tokens.
* **APR is variable.** It depends on total staked liquidity, reward emissions, and token prices.
* **Check reward periods.** Hover over reward tokens to see when emissions end.
* **CLMM rewards require in-range positions.** Out-of-range positions earn zero rewards.
