# How to bridge crypto

Already have crypto on another chain? Bridge it into Solana in a few clicks.

## Bridge into Solana

1. Go to [Raydium.io/bridge](https://Raydium.io/bridge).
2. Connect both wallets.
3. Select the source chain, destination chain, and token.
4. Confirm the transaction.

Once the transfer completes, your assets will be available on Solana.
