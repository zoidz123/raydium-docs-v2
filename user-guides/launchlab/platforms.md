# Platforms

LaunchLab’s program architecture supports third-party integration through a flexible **Platform PDA (Program Derived Address)** system. This allows external teams and platforms to create and manage their own launch environments within the LaunchLab ecosystem.

By registering a Platform PDA, third parties can offer customized launch experiences while leveraging LaunchLab’s infrastructure and Raydium’s AMM for liquidity while maintaining a separate brand and platform.

#### What Can a Platform PDA Control?

Third-party platforms using a Platform PDA can:

* Enable or disable **Creator Fee Sharing** post-bonding curves.
* Set and receive **platform-level fees** (e.g. protocol revenue share or referral cut)
* Control visual branding (name, description, logo image URL)
* Track and display all tokens launched under their platform on LaunchLab
* Set the pool fee tier post migration (if CPMM). See cpConfigId.

This setup enables ecosystems, communities, tooling providers, or meme teams to offer curated or branded token launch environments with a shared on-chain identity.
